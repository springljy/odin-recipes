Brownie Recipe

Description:

A simple way of making delicious brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 2 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray or butter.
  2. In a medium-sized saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Add the sugar and stir until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Add the cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder to the saucepan, and stir until well combined.
  4. Remove the saucepan from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  5. Add the vanilla extract and eggs to the mixture, one at a time, stirring well after each addition.
  6. Add the flour to the mixture and stir until the batter is smooth.
  7. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the brownies comes out clean.
  10. Let the brownies cool completely in the baking dish before cutting and serving.
